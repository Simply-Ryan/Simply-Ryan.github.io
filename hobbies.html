Hobbies

  • Chess

    • I have been a chess player since 2023. When I originally registered on chess.com, I had a level of approximately 800. I only play rapid and somehow lose on time in winning positions. I have made it past 1000 and my peak is at 1500. As white, I play the Ruy López when I want a win and the Scotch if I'm fine with a draw. As black, I play the Sicilian Defense, Accelerated Dragon.

  • Motorsport

    • I enjoy watching Formula 1 the most, but I find NASCAR as well as WEC very entertaining at times. My favorite drivers are Sebastian Vettel, Lewis Hamilton and Daniel Ricciardo.

  • Programming

    • I have started programming at the age of 13, in Lua for Roblox games. I found it extremely hard to understand at first, but once I got the logic it became a hobby to me. I later transitioned to C++ but couldn't wrap my mind around how to use it to make apps. I took a two year pause until now, 2024 where I am taking Harvard's CS50x and have learnt the basics of C, Python, SQL and now HTML/CSS/JavaScript.
